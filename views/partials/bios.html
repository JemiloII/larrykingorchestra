Tina Crawley

Vocals

Kathleen’s unique and varied singing career includes a recording contract with Motown records with her first band, Voyage, as well as singing backing vocals for Kenny Rogers on tour.

Jeff Morrow

Vocals

As a session singer, arranger and contractor, Jeff has worked on numerous gold and platinum albums with artists such as: R Kelly, Céline Dion, Richard Marx, Jay-Z, Ruben Studdard, Ramsey Lewis, Mavis Staples, Dennis De Young, Peter Cetera, Michael Bolton, Brian Culbertson, LaShell Griffin and many more.

John Blasucci

Vocalist/Keys

From artists like Richard Marx to Michael Bolton, John has made a living helping others share their music with the world.

Chris Siebold

Guitar

One of Chicago’s premier session and live guitar players, Chris has performed with artists such as PSYCLES, Kick the Cat, Maggie Speaks, Howard Levy and Vinnie Colaiuta to name a few.

Ethan Bender

Bass

Milwaukee born bassist Ethan Bender has been on the professional music scene since he was in his teens.

Mike Poupko

Guitar

Mike received his Jazz Performance degree from Roosevelt University in 2001 and has been performing throughout the country ever since.

Marques Carroll

Trumpet

Marques Carroll has become one of the most diverse sought-out trumpet players the city of Chicago has to offer. He can be heard soloing or leading a section for blues guitarist Guy King, The Ojays, The Temptations, The Tom Matta Big Band, The DePaul Alumni Big Band, The BMC and The Larry King Orchestra.

Nic Meyer

Saxophone & Flute

Nic attended DePaul University on a saxophone performance scholarship and received his B.M. in Music Education.

John Janowiak

Trombone

John Janowiak (a.k.a. Johnny Showtime) has carved out a niche as an in-demand trombone soloist and section man for a wide variety of groups and recording projects in and around Chicago.
