Elizabeth Huffman

Violinist




Elizabeth Huffman has performed with multiple artists and orchestras across the country and is experienced in many styles of music. Currently she holds the position of Assistant Concertmaster with the Illinois Philharmonic Orchestra, section violin with the Elgin Symphony Orchestra, and section violin with the Milwaukee Ballet Orchestra.

Elizabeth received a Bachelor of Music in Violin Performance from Western Michigan University, and a Master of Music from the Cleveland Institute of Music.
