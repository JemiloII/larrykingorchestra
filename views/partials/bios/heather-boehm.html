Heather Boehm

Violinist




Heather Boehm attended DePaul University where she received her undergraduates degree in Violin Performance and Music Business. Recent theatre credits include South Pacific,Candide, Animal Crackers, My Fair Lady, Operettas Greatest Hits, Beauty and the Beast, Grey Gardens, Caroline or Change, Carousel, and Passion.

Ms. Boehm has performed with orchestras in the Chicagoland area and throughout the country including the Charleston Symphony, Illinois Symphony, Lake Forest Symphony, New Philharmonic, Northwest Indiana Symphony, and the Chicago Chamber Orchestra. Heather has played for Barbra Streisand, Billy Joel, Linda Eder, Il Divo, Peter Gabriel, Kristin Chenoweth, Andrea Bocelli, Wayne Newton, Bobby Vinton, Celtic Woman and the Trans-Siberian Orchestra and runs a musical services company: Choice Music Chicago which partners with LKO on all string contracting and arranging needs for MFO Entertainment.

Heather as Secretary-Treasurer of the Chicago Theatre Musicians Association (TMA) and serves on committee member for the National Theatre Musicians Association.

She has served as the orchestra contractor for the Goodman Theatre's productions of The Jungle Book (2013) and Brigadoon(2014).
