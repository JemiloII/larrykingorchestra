Tina Crawley

Vocals




Tina has been singing and dancing professionally since her youth. Her credits include an appearance singing in the Celine Dion / R. Kelly “I’m Your Angel” video, performances with artists: Mavis Staples, Paul Rodgers (of Bad Company) and Ivan Neville. Tina has also recorded with the band Sonia Dada as well as many other incredible Chicago artists. Her love for Gospel, R&B and Jazz shines though in every song.
