Stephanie Harris

Violinist




Stephanie Harris was raised in Naperville Illinois where she began her violin studies at the age of four. She received her Bachelors Degree in Violin Performance from the University of Illinois on a performance scholarship where she studied with Sherban Lupu and Peter Schaeffer and served as concertmaster of the symphony.

In 2007, Stephanie was featured on tour with the preeminent international violin ensemble Barrage. National tour performances include Barry Manilow at the Rosemont Theatre, Trans-Siberian Orchestra at the Allstate Arena and Chicago Theatre, and Peter Gabriel at the United Center. She maintains an active schedule in Chicago for special events and orchestral work, including Oak Park River Forest Symphony.

In addition to her accomplished performance career, Ms. Harris is a chiropractic physician. In 2012 she opened her own practice: Harrison Street Chiropractic in the Oak Park Arts District, where she specializes in treating repetitive motion injuries in musicians.
